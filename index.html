Privacy Policy

How JunkYardMechs handles player data across the Meta platform, PlayFab, and Photon — what's collected, why, and how to get it deleted.

LAST UPDATED — JUNE 2026 // APPLIES TO META QUEST BUILD
Data We Collect How We Use It Deletion & Account Removal Third-Party Services User Profile Children's Privacy

01Information We Collect

This app collects and processes limited user data provided by the Meta platform and the third-party services used to operate the game. No real-world personal information — real name, email address, or physical address — is collected by the app itself.

User ID
Provided by the Meta platform. Used to uniquely identify players for account functionality, multiplayer features, and entitlement validation.
Age Group
Provided by the Meta platform. Used to determine age-appropriate access to features and to comply with platform safety requirements. The app does not collect exact age or date of birth.
PlayFab Player Data
Tracks player progression, statistics, and account-related game data.
Photon Networking Data
Session and connection data required to provide real-time multiplayer server connectivity.
Purchase & Entitlement Data
Used to verify purchases, unlock downloadable content, and restore previously purchased items.

02How We Use the Data

The collected data is used only to:

  • Enable core gameplay features
  • Support multiplayer and online services
  • Save player progress and game statistics
  • Validate in-app purchases and downloadable content
  • Enforce age-appropriate access to features
  • Comply with Meta platform policies

User data is not sold, shared for advertising, or used for tracking outside of gameplay functionality.

03Data Deletion & Account Removal

Player data associated with this app is managed through PlayFab.

  • Uninstalling the game stops all further data collection.
  • Stored gameplay data may remain associated with your account unless deletion is requested.

You may request deletion of your stored account data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all PlayFab account data associated with your user account will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by platform policy or law.

Data Deletion Contact yuzadeveloper@gmail.com

04Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services to operate core functionality. These services only process data necessary to provide gameplay features and do not use the data for advertising purposes.

  • PlayFab — player accounts, progression, and statistics
  • Photon — multiplayer server networking

05User Profile

Your profile image is used to display your avatar for in-game identity, multiplayer recognition, and social features. The image is only used for identification inside the app and is not sold or used for advertising.

06Children's Privacy

This app relies on Meta's User Age Group classification and does not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is provided by the platform and required for gameplay.
